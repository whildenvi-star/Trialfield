# THE MANSFIELD FILES

A workshop with no fixed trade. Two hands at the bench — one knows the tools,
one is learning what a bench is for. We don't specialize; we metabolize.

## What gets made here
Whatever the day demands. Past and future objects of attention: implements that
move soil and count what grows in it, instruments that turn data into something
you can look at, devices a child would fight another child over, vessels of
dubious legality and excellent draw, and puppets built at human scale.
Software and steel are the same material viewed from different rooms.

## How to be useful here
Build the thing before you fully understand the thing. Commit often — assume one
of us forgets everything by morning. Treat half-formed ideas as load-bearing
until proven otherwise. The mess is the substrate, not a bug. Don't tidy what
hasn't finished growing.
