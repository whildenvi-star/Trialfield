<objective>
Wire organic-cert seed compilation to read lot numbers, cert numbers, OMRI status, and supplier names from seed-inventory instead of farm-budget.

Purpose: Eliminate double-entry of seed data. Seed-inventory already tracks delivered seed lots with lot numbers and organic cert numbers from receipts. Organic-cert currently pulls seed variety/brand from farm-budget's seed catalog which lacks lot-level compliance data. This rewires the compilation to use seed-inventory as the authoritative source for NOP seed compliance.

Output: Enhanced seed-inventory API, updated organic-cert client and seed-mapper that uses seed-inventory as primary seed data source.
</objective>
