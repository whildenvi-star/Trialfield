  <how-to-verify>
    **On your phone (or Chrome DevTools mobile emulation at 375px):**

    1. Open portal.whughesfarms.com/dashboard
       - PASS: You see a vertical list of module cards (Field Ops, Field History, etc.)
       - FAIL: You see only the FieldMap, a blank page, or a crash

    2. Scroll the card list
       - PASS: Cards are readable, text is not clipped, touch targets feel large enough
       - FAIL: Cards overflow horizontally, text wraps badly, buttons are too small to tap

    3. Find the Field Ops card — look for a "Done" button next to a pending pass
       - PASS: Button is present and tappable; after tap the pass disappears/changes to confirmed without navigating away
       - FAIL: No button visible, or tapping navigates to a different page

    4. Tap the body of any card (not the button)
       - PASS: Navigates into that module's full page
       - FAIL: Nothing happens, or wrong page

    5. Open portal.whughesfarms.com/dashboard on a desktop/laptop browser
       - PASS: You see the existing FieldMap (not cards)
       - FAIL: FieldMap is gone, or both cards and map show at the same time

    6. Log in as a crew/operator account (if available) and check /dashboard
       - PASS: Fewer cards are shown (no financial/admin modules)
       - FAIL: All cards visible regardless of role
